About us

Welcome to Freshly Inn, a classic Moscow restaurant, owned by legendary restaurateur Timothy B. Here the charm of old Moscow meets 21st century modernity. In a historic building that has housed a destination restaurant since the 1930s, Freshly Inn today welcomes a convivial and urbane clientele.

Freshly Inn offers superb contemporary Modern cuisine, warm, polished service and beautiful rooms suitable for a wide range of events.

Private dining

Elegant and timeless, Freshly Inn features four private dining areas that offer real versatility, whether you are having an intimate birthday dinner for friends or using the entire townhouse for a wedding celebration or corporate event.

Make new memories in our unforgettable private dining rooms. With options for lunches, presentations, seated dinners, or cocktail receptions, private dining is the perfect opportunity to create your own personal midtown oasis.

Whether you are nestled in one of our more intimate rooms for a celebratory dinner or taking over our entire restaurant for a wedding or special occasion, Freshly Inn is the perfect setting for a unique, undeniably Moscow event.
